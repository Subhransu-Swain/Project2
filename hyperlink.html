Mission

Silicon Institute of Technology (SIT) Bhubaneswar is a private college established in the year 2001. The college
has been granted autonomous status by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and approved by the All India
Council for Technical Education (AICTE). SIT Bhubaneswar is affiliated to Biju Patnaik University of Technology,
Rourkela. The institute has been accredited by the NAAC with an ‘A’ grade. The campus of the college is spread
over an area of 19 acres. As per the official website, the college vision is to provide technical education and
develop future leaders. The institute has secured 163rd rank in the Engineering category in NIRF 2021 Ranking
with an overall score of 34.46 out of 100. The institute had secured 179th rank in the same category in NIRF
2020 Ranking.
The institute offers admission in B.Tech, M.Tech. and M.C.A. programmes. The B.Tech programme is offered in six
specialisations and the M.Tech. programme is offered in three specialisations. The National Body of
Accreditation (NBA) has accredited three specialisations of the B.Tech course. Candidates' scores in the JEE
Main are accepted for admission in the B.Tech course. On the other hand, Candidates admission to M.Tech. and
M.C.A. courses are done based on the OJEE score. Silicon Institute of Technology Bhubaneswar also accepts GATE
score for M.Tech. admission. To take admission candidates need to participate in the OJEE counselling.

About Us

The Silicon Institute of Technology, Bhubaneswar (SIT-BBSR) is a NAAC Grade A grade engineering institution with
NIRF 2021 rank of 163 in Bhubaneswar, the capital of Odisha, India. Established in 2001 as an affiliated college
of the Utkal University, the college has been an affiliated college of Biju Patnaik University of Technology,
since 2002. Students are admitted to the college through the Joint Entrance Examination (main) merit list of
Government of India. The college is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation and was granted autonomy
by University Grants Commission (UGC) in 2017 for a period of 10 years. In 2009, Silicon Institute of
Technology, Sambalpur was established as its sister institution.
